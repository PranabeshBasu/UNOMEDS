Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

We follow HIPAA-aware design principles and minimize data retention. Your uploads remain private to your account.

Can UNOMEDS replace a doctor?

No. We provide guidance and next steps, not a diagnosis. If you experience red-flag symptoms, seek medical care immediately.

What documents can I upload?

Prescriptions, lab results (PDF), and medical reports. We’ll expand supported formats soon.

How fast is the response?

Typical responses are under 30 seconds depending on load, with a target accuracy rate of 98% in supported tasks.
